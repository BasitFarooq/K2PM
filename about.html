Welcome to Kids2ProSports

Kids2ProSports, (t/a Kids2Pro.com and Kids2ProSports.com) which is currently being developed to offer the world's first interactive, multicultural Sports Legacy Search Engine chronicling the journey of athletic competition from childhood to...professional...and beyond.

A passionate family opportunity to create your children's sports legacy nurturing their social maturation into their professional and personal futures, while also providing families the ability to share their sport memories. The Portal will feature over 1,000 sport categories; over 100,000 definitive sport and health terms, and ancestry applications with supportive sports news content submitted by members; sport literacy, health education, sport skills training and peer interactivity. You will be able to create multiple sport profiles and personal management accounts to submit opinions, upload videos, promote personal sport activities and generate future professional, business and educational opportunities in your languages of preference.
