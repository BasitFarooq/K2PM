Kids2ProSports.com – Your Introduction to the World of Sports Recognition

K2PS is a virtual global networking community designed to provide a variety of services to educate, support, and nurture athletes of all ages.

We all have experienced, spending countless hours and money providing the fundamental tools that we believe our children need to succeed in their sports career. K2PS was developed based on the belief that everyone should have an equal opportunity by offering the tools to take those efforts to the next level; to help gain a local2global audience to showcase and compare our member's sport skills and create unlimited sport, educational and professional opportunities.

There is an expression "The World Is Your Stage",but not if no one knows about you.

In our 24-hour sport news cycle, reputation and image is an important aspect of an athlete's success, K2PS was developed to give athletes the opportunity to proactively manage their image and to assist in their athletic branding in the sport landscape.

K2PS provides the most comprehensive source for industry connections, featuring 360 social network options; 75 national & international sport groups with over 1.3 million members and support of over 10 million associates; organizations whose memberships represent over 80 million athletes; thousands of college coaches and millions of sport enthusiasts and sport related professionals.

The United States is a melting pot of cultures from around the world, and the sports its athletes participate in reflect that variety.

Through this cross-generational Sport Legacy Search Engine, athletes can create a personal game plan specific to their vision for personal achievement, broadening their appeal on the road to success and increased opportunities as a public sports figure, balancing academic excellence and sports participation.
