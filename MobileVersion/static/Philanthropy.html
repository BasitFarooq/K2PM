Kids2ProSports.com Sports Philanthropic Mission

K2PS Sports Philanthropy

Because everyone deserves a chance, The Kids2ProSports Philanthropic mission is to establish opportunities for dynamic, philanthropic individuals and organizations involved in the world of sports and to share those possibilities with its members.

Diversity

Embracing diversity and encouraging multicultural outreach efforts is essential to achieving the K2PS’ Sports Philanthropy’s mission. K2PS is committed to fostering a cross-generational environment that is more inclusive and inviting to all people in working to expand the image of athletes from around the world.

What We Do

The K2PS SP works to maximize the impact that professional sports leagues, teams and athletes can have in creating healthier lifestyles for America’s youth and families.

The K2PS Sports Philanthropy mission (K2PS SP) works to maximize the impact that professional sports leagues, teams and athletes can have in impacting social need in our society. From areas as diverse as health, to youth development, to educational initiatives —the sports industry possesses elements which are unparalleled in effecting change when deployed strategically.

K2PS SP does not accept donations and is only supplying the platform for members who would like to participate in sport charities and philanthropic organizations. We welcome your consideration to any of our members represented in over 1,000 world sports who may be in need of your generosity. It is strongly recommended that before you make any commitment to anyone that you complete a thorough due diligence.

K2PS SP does not endorse or have any related interest in the organizations listed and does not receive any compensation for its services or introductions.

K2PS SP is not a grant-making organization, and we're unable to respond to requests for support. However, if you're a grant seeker, one resource which you may find helpful is our Sports Foundations Directory.. The Directory includes profiles of almost 400 philanthropic entities associated with professional sports leagues, teams and athletes across all major sports in the U.S. and Canada. Please feel free to make use of this resource. If you do not see an organization listed, please submit the information to be included to K2PSSP.com. Please note that any inclusion of additional organizations will be further identified as you being the contributing member.

As a supporter of Sports Philanthropy, K2PS Sports Philanthropy will donate a percentage of its net advertising revenue to sport related charities and philanthropic organizations.

Thank you for your continued support,
