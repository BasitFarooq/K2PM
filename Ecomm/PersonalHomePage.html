Creating Your Own Sports Home Page

K2PS is offering personalized sport home page opportunities for its members where you can select a sport and sport category from over 15,000 sport selections or create a personal identification of your own from our 2.5million sport terms and keywords. This option will be available in April 2012. To indicate your interest in having any of these opportunities created for you, please send your request to support@k2ps.com. Include your name, personal sport page feature(s) and contact information. We will notify you as soon as these features are operational.

K2PS offers the following three options.

Option (1) Members have the option of having personal identified sport home pages by name identification only. (Ex. Jeff Gamer; ABC Sport Doctors, etc.). This option allows other members to submit content to your site. Members can select one or multiple home pages. You can use our contact importation; invite a friend and the option of sharing with over 360 social networks. Please note: These personalized home page options will be identical in format to the home pages that exist on our site with the exception of the name change. If you need assistance please contact support@k2ps.com

This option allows you to submit content but does not allow any personal administrative controls as noted in Option 2.
