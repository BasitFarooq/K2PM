Welcome to Kids2ProSports.com

Kids2ProSports is the world’s premier multicultural, global sports network chronicling your sports legacy from childhood…. to professional and beyond.

For Kids and Adults of all Ages, our members enjoy the ability to share their sport memories and create multiple sport and sport related profiles in their languages of choice. For families, the opportunity to create, preserve and share in their children’s sports legacy, nurturing their social life skills

Brand Yourself
This cross-generational sport platform empowers athletes to "Brand" and enhance their image through their choice from over 30,000 USA and global sport selections.

Build Your Legacy

Kids2ProSports offers the resources needed to advance athletes, other sport related professionals and enthusiasts at any age specific to the sports they are participating in. The goal is to progress this athlete’s level of play over their entire lifetime, providing them more opportunities than anywhere on the Internet. Members can utilize our numerous programs to share and enhance their sports accomplishments…. locally, nationally and worldwide.

Create & Share

At K2PS you can stay in touch with the people you shared some of the most memorable sport moments of your life. Best of all, K2PS is a safe community for athletes of all ages, to share their interest and passions while discovering those of others. With our exclusive criteria peer search, athletes will be able to compare themselves to athletes from their community.... . to around the world in their respective sports where over 2.3 billion participate in some form of sports activity.

Be Challenged

K2PS is where you need to be to compete and showcase your accomplishments in a sports market where in the United States with a population approaching 311 million has 90% of its youth participate in some form of sport activity; 23.6 million people play some form of professional sports and there are 68 million performance athletes and 55 million fitness athletes. Baby boomers (ages 50 – 74) are a generation of 78 million strong and their interest is an active lifestyle in sports and fitness…Be competitive...be prepared for the challenge!

Philantrophy

In support of sport competition, K2PS will donate a portion of its advertising revenue to sport related charities, philanthropic efforts, grants and scholarships.
