Kids2ProSports.com –Your Introduction to the World of Sports Recognition

K2PS is a virtual networking community designed to provide a variety of services and to educate, support, and nurture athletes of all ages and provide access to a global audience.

The United States is a melting pot of cultures from around the world, and the sports its athletes participate in reflect that variety.

Some Facts:

  1. The current US population is almost 310.6 million and 90% of the youth in the USA participate in some form of sport activity. (*).
  2. There are 23.6 million people in the United States who play some form of professional sports. (**).
  3. The National Council of Youth Sports membership has over 44,000,000 boys & girls who are participating in youth sports. (***).
  4. Gatorade’s research indicates there are 68 million performance athletes and 55 million fitness athletes. (****).
  5. There are 7 billion human being and 2.3 billion participate in some form of sport activity. Also bold: 2.3 billion participate in some form of sport activity. (*).

We all have experienced, spending countless hours and money providing the fundamental tools that we believe our children need to succeed in their sports career. Kids2ProSports was developed based on the belief that everyone should have an equal opportunity and it offers the tools to chronicle their sports legacy from childhood…to professional and beyond and help gain a global audience to showcase and compare their sport skills and create unlimited sport and professional opportunities.

There is an expression “The World Is Your Stage”, but not if no one knows about you.

K2PS offers additional options in public relations, branding, article submissions, contact introductions, press releases and alternative promotional services for its members.

K2PS provides the most comprehensive selection of 1,500 world sports, 250,000 keywords/categories and 360 social networks on the Internet. K2SP offers personalized services connecting to our 70 national & international sports groups with over 1.3Million contacts and support of over 10 million associated connections; Sport organizations and sport enthusiasts representing over 50 million athletes, 40,000 college coaches and athletic directors and thousands of sport & entertainment related professionals.

K2PS’ platform will balance the pursuit of academic excellence and sports participation.

We are here for you…..join our sports community and let us introduce you the world of sports recognition, competition and connections.

For a personal analysis of your goals and how we may help, please contact us at Resources@k2ps.com
