Kids2ProSports – Education & Sports

Kids2ProSports Academic & Sports mission is to focus on educational attainment and using the power and appeal of sport to positively affect our members by supporting physical and personal education, sport, and physical activity programs.

Kids2ProSports.com is a unique web based company that emphasizes dedicated synergy of academics and sport within an international environment. We strongly believe that a combination of intellectual pursuits with the physical, notably as realized through sports in all its varied forms, continually enhances our lives at every level of our growth from childhood…to professional and beyond.

Our lives are a continuum of learning, change and adaptability accompanied by the quest to succeed. It requires communication and discipline so our kids can play and have fun, creating life long memories. As our site and business evolves with an increasing audience, we invite you to grow along with us and become a member of our community.

Thanks for your support and we look forward to hearing from you.

American Association for Physical Activity and Recreation (www.aahperd.org) (AAPAR) links professionals in education with community and agency-based programs to facilitate ongoing education for professionals providing physical activity, recreation, and fitness programs across the lifespan. AAPAR supports a broad spectrum of issues and populations including aquatics, adapted physical activity, outdoor recreation, facility design and management, fitness for older adults, and safety and risk management.

National Association for Girls and Women in Sport (www.aahperd.org) (NAGWS) is the force behind Title IX and has advocated for girls and women for more than a century. NAGWS is the leading organization for equity issues in sports and provides information and programming for all administrators and teachers working with girls and women.

The National Association for Sport and Physical Education (www.aahperd.org) (NASPE) sets the standard for excellence in physical education, physical activity and sport and offers a broad array of informational conferences, professional development opportunities, books and cutting edge resources to help you become a leader in your classroom, school, and community. The National Dance Association (www.aahperd.org) (NDA) leads the way in advocating for quality dance programs at all levels in the arts and physical education. NDA produces essential resources to develop and market quality dance programs and important networking opportunities to advance careers in dance education.
